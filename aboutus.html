A little bit about us...

Based in the heart of Galway city, Vintage Rework first opened it's doors in 1999 and has since been a haven for vintage shoppers from all over the country. Selling the best re-worked vintage clothing, shoes and accesories at the best prices. With a only small number of staff, we work very hard to make sure our customers find the best pieces to suit their individual style. Even if it's just to have a browse, we'll make sure your experience is a pleasant one.
